Instructions

The Ivy Lee Method

  • Step 1

    At the end of the day write 6 tasks you need to do tomorrow

  • Step 2

    Prioritize tasks in order of importance

  • Step 3

    The next day start with the most important task and work on it until you finish it

  • Step 4

    The, repeat with every task on your list

  • Step 5

    Do this everyday
